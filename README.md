# Fitness-Buddy-AI-Agentic

## Overview

Fitness Buddy AI is an Agentic AI-powered fitness assistant designed to help users achieve their health and wellness goals through personalized workout plans, nutrition guidance, progress tracking, and motivational support.

Built using IBM Granite Models, IBM watsonx.ai, and LangFlow, the system combines multiple AI agents to provide intelligent, adaptive, and context-aware fitness recommendations.

---

## Problem Statement

Many individuals struggle to maintain consistent fitness routines, balanced nutrition, and healthy lifestyles due to the lack of personalized guidance and motivation. Fitness information is often scattered across various sources, making it difficult to create effective workout and nutrition plans.

Fitness Buddy AI addresses these challenges by providing a smart virtual fitness coach that delivers personalized recommendations, tracks progress, and adapts to user needs.

## Agentic AI Architecture

### Fitness Knowledge Agent

Retrieves exercise and fitness information from trusted sources.

### Workout Planner Agent

Creates customized workout routines based on user goals.

### Nutrition Advisor Agent

Provides meal recommendations and calorie guidance.

### Progress Tracking Agent

Monitors workouts, calories burned, and fitness improvements.

### Motivation & Wellness Agent

Offers reminders, wellness tips, and motivational support.
